About CityFix

CityFix is a community-driven platform that allows citizens to report and track local issues like potholes, sewage leaks, and traffic congestion. Our goal is to bridge the gap between the public and local authorities, ensuring a cleaner, safer, and more efficient city.

Our Mission

To empower citizens with a voice to address everyday urban issues and create a more responsive governance system.

Our Vision

To build smarter cities where public concerns are acknowledged and resolved efficiently, making urban living better for everyone.
