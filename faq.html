Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is CityFix? +
CityFix is a platform where users can register complaints about daily societal issues like potholes, sewage problems, and traffic concerns and track their status.
2. How do I register a complaint? +
You can register a complaint by signing up on CityFix, providing the issue details, and submitting the complaint. You will receive a tracking ID for updates.
3. Can I track my complaint status? +
Yes, CityFix allows users to track the status of their complaints using their unique tracking ID.
4. Is CityFix available for all cities? +
Currently, CityFix is being implemented in selected cities. We aim to expand our services to more locations in the future.
5. Is there a mobile app for CityFix? +
As of now, CityFix is accessible via a web platform. A mobile app is under development and will be launched soon.
