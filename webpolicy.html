CityFix Website Policy

1. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Information like your name, email, and complaint details are stored locally in your browser and are not shared with any third parties.

2. Data Storage

CityFix uses local storage to save user and complaint data. This means your data is only saved in your browser and not uploaded anywhere online. Clearing your browser data will remove your saved complaints.

3. User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate and appropriate complaint details. Offensive, false, or spammy content in complaints or discussions is discouraged and may be removed by admins.

4. Security Measures

Only registered users can access complaint and discussion features. Please keep your login details secure and do not share them with others.

5. Cookies

CityFix does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to enhance user clarity and experience. Any major changes will be communicated to users.
